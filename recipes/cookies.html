Iced Pumpkin Cookies

Description

"These pumpkin cookies are tender and soft. The icing is always a hit! Both kids and adults love them!" - Allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Spices
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Sugars
  • Butter
  • Pumpkin
  • Egg
  • Vanilla
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Make the cookie dough.
  2. Drop the dough by the tablespoonful onto a cookie sheet and flatten slightly.
  3. Bake until the centers are set, then cool on a wire rack.
  4. Make the icing and drizzle it over the cooled cookies.
